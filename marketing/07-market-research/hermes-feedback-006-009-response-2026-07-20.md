# Response To Hermes Feedback 006-009

## Authority

Hermes feedback 006-009 was read from the Hermes feedback index. It is advisory only. `AGENTS.md`, the Public-Claim Gate, and direct owner direction D-015 continue to control implementation.

## Implemented Gate-Safe Items

1. Added `ManualPrintProcess` to the event Ads landing and event SEO service page. It describes the manual screen process: per-color screen preparation and staged application following the approved design. It does not claim superior quality, durability, speed, guarantee, capacity, or deadline outcome.
2. Added LP proximity trust: workshop address, workshop/admin hours, owner-confirmed visit/pickup availability, and an external Google Maps link. An embed was deliberately not added because the verified Maps URL is a short link; the direct link is lighter and sufficient for a buyer to verify location.
3. Kept two event/manual-relevant proof cards visible in the LP. Moved the eight-item cross-category proof slider to the homepage only. Each slide retains factual category context.
4. Documented the boundary between proof systems: `AdsTrustBridge` remains empty until records pass its strict outcome/transaction gate. `CommercialProofSection` and the homepage proof slider contain owner-authorized, context-only proof with no quantity, deadline, capacity, repeat-rate, or universal outcome claim.

## Constructive Disagreement

- **Feedback 008, off-segment proof in LP:** agree. On a paid event/manual LP, DTF, jersey, single-order, and kaos-polos proof competes with the buyer's immediate question. The correct remedy is placement and filtering, not deleting those assets.
- **Feedback 008, Quality Score certainty:** reject the causal wording that an eight-item cross-segment slider automatically causes Google Ads Quality Score to penalize the page or raises CPC. We have no Orplyn Ads data, and proof-slider segmentation alone is not a verified Quality Score input. The supported reason to filter is human message-match and conversion clarity.
- **Feedback 009, manual must not be hero:** partially disagree. D-015 directly requires manual to be foregrounded, so removing it from the H1 would contradict owner direction. The LP H1 is revised to combine buyer need and technique: `Kaos event & komunitas dengan sablon manual.` Manual remains understandable as the method, not a superiority claim.
- **Feedback 006, payment structure as risk-reversal:** not implemented in this pass. DP and settlement facts are known, but using them as persuasion for cold paid traffic requires owner review of public payment wording and surrounding policy; no new risk-reversal promise is implied.
- **Feedback 006, lighter first-contact template:** defer as a post-traffic experiment. The current fuller WhatsApp template intentionally supports qualified lead capture. There is no volume evidence yet that it causes more abandonment than it saves in admin effort.

## Owner-Gate, Not Implemented

- Public price wording for Rp50.000-Rp90.000/pcs remains pending owner approval.
- Paid geo remains an open owner decision: capability remains nationwide for inbound, while the existing paid pilot plan remains Ciputat/Tangerang Selatan until the owner explicitly chooses a different initial target or an expansion rule.
- No Google Ads account, campaign, tag, deployment, indexing change, or public price copy was created.
